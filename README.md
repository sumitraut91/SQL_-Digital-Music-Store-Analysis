🎵 Digital Music Store Analysis (SQL Project)

📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing an online digital music store database using PostgreSQL. 

Through this project, we analyze customer behavior, sales trends, and music preferences to help the store improve its growth strategy.

🎯 Objectives

Understand the structure of a relational database

Practice writing SQL queries (Basic → Advanced)

Perform data analysis using joins, aggregations, and subqueries

Generate business insights from raw data

🛠️ Tools & Technologies

Database: PostgreSQL

Interface: pgAdmin 4

Language: SQL

📂 Dataset Description

The dataset represents a digital music store and includes the following key tables:

Customer – Customer details

Invoice – Purchase transactions

Invoice Line – Track-level purchase details

playlist_track

playlist

Track – Song information

Album – Album details

Artist – Artist information

Genre – Music categories

Employee – Staff details

❓ Business Questions Solved

Who is the senior-most employee based on job title?

Which countries have the most invoices?

What are the top 3 invoice totals?

Which city generates the highest revenue?

Who is the best customer (highest spender)?

List all Rock music listeners (email, name, genre)

Top 10 artists with the most Rock tracks

Tracks longer than the average song length
